FIRST LEGO League

2014 - Present | Total Hours: 48 (and counting)

Each year I support my local FIRST LEGO League chapter by helping to host the regional qualifier tournament. My role is to set up all the tech equipment, including audio, computers, tablets, servers, projectors, and power. I also control the music and act as the scorekeeper, inputting recorded scores from judges in real-time to be displayed on the scoreboards.
